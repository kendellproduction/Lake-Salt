Transparent Pricing

How Pricing Works

Every event is unique — so is every quote. Here's exactly how we build yours.

1
Tell us about your event
Share your event type, date, guest count, duration, and venue. The more detail, the more accurate your quote — and the more we can personalize your bar experience.
2
We build your custom quote
We factor in bartender count, service hours, bar setup complexity, specialty cocktail menus, and any add-ons. Your quote reflects exactly what you need — nothing you don't.
3
You receive your shopping list
Because we're dry hire, you purchase the alcohol. We send a precise shopping list — every spirit, wine, and beer — so there's zero guesswork or wasted spend.
4
We show up, you celebrate
Our TIPS-certified, food handler certified team arrives early, sets up, serves professionally all night, and handles full breakdown. You never lift a finger.
What shapes your quote
Every element below is considered when we build your personalized estimate. No hidden fees, no surprises — just honest pricing for a premium experience.
