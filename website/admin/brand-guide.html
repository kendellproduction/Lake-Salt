Brand Identity

Voice & Tone

Warm and sophisticated — never stuffy. We bring craft and care to every event, whether it's an intimate backyard wedding or a 500-person corporate gala.

We Are

  • Elegant without being pretentious
  • Inclusive — every guest matters, mocktails included
  • Professional and prepared, always
  • Warm, personal, and detail-obsessed

We Are Not

  • Generic or corporate in tone
  • Alcohol-pushy — we celebrate both
  • Overly casual or gimmicky
  • Transactional — we build relationships
