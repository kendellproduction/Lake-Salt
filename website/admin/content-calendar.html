Content Pillar Strategy

Our content strategy revolves around five core pillars that drive engagement and bookings. Each pillar serves a distinct purpose in the customer journey while maintaining brand consistency.

🍸

Behind the Bar

Your hero content. Drink-making videos, recipe reveals, and bartender tips. Shot from overhead or close-up POV with hands-only framing. Voiceover explains ingredients and technique. This is your most engaging content—focus here to build audience.

🎉

Event Highlights

Before/after setup photos, corporate event recaps, client testimonials, and event day moments. Shows the energy and professionalism of your service. Builds social proof and demonstrates real-world execution. Great for converting interested leads.

📋

Menu Spotlights

Showcase signature cocktails and mocktails. Recipe cards, ingredient breakdowns, seasonal menu features. Perfect for static posts and carousel content. Emphasize mocktails as a key differentiator. Use Canva designs for polished graphics.

🏔️

Utah Local

Utah-specific content, venue partnerships, local event tie-ins, and seasonal celebrations. Tap into Utah pride and Wasatch Front community. Seasonal content (spring weddings, ski season entertaining, holiday parties). Builds local relevance and SEO.

📲

Book With Us

Direct CTAs for booking, pricing teasers, process explainers, FAQ content, and social proof. These drive conversions. Include link-in-bio buttons, booking process visuals, and frequently answered questions. Balance with other content (aim for 20% of posts).

Drink-Making Video Guide

Your hero content. Hands-only drink videos with voiceover are your most engaging posts. Here's how to create them like a pro.

📱 Platform Specs & Video Length

TikTok (Your growth engine)

  • Ideal Length: 15-30 seconds (hook fast, show the payoff)
  • Resolution: 1080x1920 (vertical)
  • Format: Use trending sounds + text overlays for captions
  • Hook: First 3 seconds are CRITICAL—show the finished drink or an eye-catching pour

Instagram Reels

  • Ideal Length: 30-60 seconds (longer format allows more detail)
  • Resolution: 1080x1920 (vertical) or 1080x1350 (square)
  • Format: Original audio (builds brand recognition) + captions
  • Pacing: Slower, more sophisticated than TikTok. Show technique.

Facebook

  • Ideal Length: 20-45 seconds (shorter attention span)
  • Format: Auto-play with captions (most watch without sound)
  • Hook: Text overlay explaining the drink in first 2 seconds

🎥 Shooting Setup & Angles

Overhead Shot (Flat Lay)

  • Setup: Mount phone on tripod or prop it at 45° angle pointing down
  • Lighting: Natural window light is perfect. Avoid harsh shadows.
  • What to show: Pouring, stirring, shaking, adding garnish. The whole process head-on.
  • Best for: Showing technique, layering, garnishing details

Side Angle (45°)

  • Setup: Phone at eye level, angled at 45° to the bar
  • Why it works: You see both the bartender's hands and the glass/bottle in profile
  • Best for: Dramatic pours, shaking, drink transformations

Close-Up (Macro)

  • Setup: Phone 6-12 inches from the action. Focus on drink only.
  • What to show: Liquid colors mixing, ice clinking, garnish placement, final presentation
  • Best for: Visual appeal, showing quality ingredients, satisfying transitions

Pro Tip: Shoot Multiple Angles

Make one drink 3-4 times from different angles. Edit together the best shots for maximum production value without extra work.

🎙️ Voiceover Script Framework

Use this simple structure. Each section should be 5-10 seconds of audio. Speak clearly, naturally, with energy.

[0-3 sec] HOOK / INTRO "This is our [Drink Name]. [1-2 sentence about what makes it special]" [3-8 sec] INGREDIENTS "We start with [base spirit], add [mixer], a touch of [liqueur/modifier]..." [8-15 sec] TECHNIQUE "Shake it up... add ice... beautiful color... [describe what they're seeing]..." [15-18 sec] GARNISH "Top it with [garnish]. That's the final touch that makes it special." [18-22 sec] CTA "Want this at your next event? Book us on lakesalt.us or check the link in our bio. Lake Salt—Utah's mobile bar."

Voice Tips

  • Speak slowly and clearly—your audience is watching, not just listening
  • Use conversational tone, not robotic
  • Emphasize key ingredients and steps
  • End with confidence: "Book us at lakesalt.us" (not a question)
  • Optional: Add background music under voiceover, but keep VO audible

🎵 Audio & Music Strategy

TikTok & Reels: Trending Sounds

  • Use 2-4 trending sounds per month—algorithm LOVES this
  • Search sounds related to: "bartender," "satisfying," "mixing," "drinks"
  • Layer your voiceover ON TOP of trending audio (don't mute it)
  • Time your key moments (pour, clink, garnish) to beat drops

Original Audio (Brand Building)

  • Record your voiceover without music first
  • Use original audio occasionally—when algorithm picks it up, it becomes YOUR branded audio
  • This is how you build a signature Lake Salt sound

Music Library Suggestions

  • Upbeat/Fun: "Chill Vibes," "Lo-Fi Hip Hop," "Indie Pop"
  • Sophisticated: "Jazz," "Lounge," "Ambient"
  • Energetic: "Electronic," "Synthwave," "Disco"
  • Tools: YouTube Audio Library (free), Epidemic Sound, Artlist

✂️ Editing & Publishing

Simple Editing (You can do this)

  • Cuts: Trim boring moments. Keep it punchy.
  • Speed Ramp: Slow down on garnish, speed up the pouring—creates visual interest
  • Text Overlay: Add ingredient names as they appear (e.g., "+vodka" when you add vodka)
  • Final Sip/Reveal: End with 2-3 seconds of the finished drink. HOLD ON IT.

Editing Tools

  • Easy (phone): CapCut (free, powerful, best for TikTok/Reels)
  • Medium (desktop): DaVinci Resolve (free), Adobe Premiere Elements (paid)
  • Quick: Use CapCut to trim + add captions + post directly

Publishing Strategy

  • TikTok First: Post here first for algorithmic advantage
  • Then Reels: Cross-post to Instagram (within 24 hours)
  • Then Facebook: Share to your Facebook page with a caption
  • Timing: Post Tuesday/Thursday 6-9 PM or Wednesday 6 PM (peak Utah engagement)

🚀 Making Videos That Convert

Engagement Drivers

  • Satisfying action: Smooth pours, ice clinking, garnish placement
  • Color contrast: Show when ingredients mix and change color
  • Before/After: Empty glass → full glass with garnish
  • Storytelling: "This is for a client who wanted something special..."

Posting Checklist

  • ☑ Hook in first 3 seconds
  • ☑ Clear voiceover describing what you're doing
  • ☑ Text captions for key steps (ingredient names, techniques)
  • ☑ 2-3 camera angles mixed (overhead → side → close-up)
  • ☑ Trending sound or original audio with VO layered over
  • ☑ Strong CTA: "Book us at lakesalt.us or link in bio"
  • ☑ Relevant hashtags from your strategy
  • ☑ Pinned comment with booking link or recipe in comments
